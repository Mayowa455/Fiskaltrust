Microsoft Teams Training Program

As a new or existing user of Microsoft Teams within fiskaltrust, I want access to a structured training program, so that I can understand how to effectively use Teams for communication, collaboration, and integration with other tools.

Program Overview & Documentation

This comprehensive training program is designed to guide users through all facets of Microsoft Teams, from quick-start basics to advanced AI-driven features. Each module contains clear learning objectives, video-based topics, and explanations of why the training matters to you and fiskaltrust.

How to use this guide: Navigate modules in sequence or jump to topics of interest. Click on any video link to watch the official Microsoft training clip.
